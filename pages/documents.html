Proclamation of Independence (1971)

Publication date: 26 March 1971

Summary: The proclamation announces the establishment of an independent state and sets out the legal and moral justification for independence. It was widely circulated by radio and through leaflets and served as a rallying document for the liberation movement.

Source: National archives / contemporary press releases.

Instrument of Surrender (1971)

Publication date: 16 December 1971

Summary: The Instrument of Surrender documents the formal capitulation that ended major hostilities. The original signed text records the terms and the parties present.

Source: Military archives / declassified diplomatic files.

Delhi Agreement / Tripartite Notes

Publication date: 1972 (various)

Summary: A set of communications and agreements recorded in the immediate post-conflict period addressing repatriation, administration, and diplomatic recognition.

Source: Foreign ministry records; diplomatic cables.
